Cocker Spaniel

Cocker Spaniels can be trained to take care of their owners or simply be there when you need a snuggle

  • Scientific name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average lifespan: 12-15 years
  • Habitat: with their companions
  • Average length: 12-16 inches

Cocker Spaniels are cuddly and affectionate pets. They provide comfort to their owners. They are very intellgent which makes them excellent to train.
